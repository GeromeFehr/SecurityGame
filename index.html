Game Premise

A modern IT-security style built for a game, not a boring corporate page.

The game puts you inside a lively office environment where employees constantly create avoidable risks. You move in first person, read the room, reach the danger fast, and stop incidents with smart reactions.

Core Loop
  • Patrol the office floor and look for suspicious behavior
  • Catch real-world security failures created by NPCs
  • Resolve incidents before time runs out
  • Build score, maintain compliance, prevent chaos
Systems

Gameplay systems that fit the concept

01

First-Person Patrol

Explore the building naturally with mouse and keyboard, instead of point-and-click menus.

02

Dynamic NPC Mistakes

Employees trigger real security issues such as phishing, bad USB usage or unlocked devices.

03

Readable Threat UX

Strong visual markers and modern UI help the player instantly understand where the danger is.

04

Awareness Through Play

The concept works as an actual game and as a security-awareness experience for companies.
